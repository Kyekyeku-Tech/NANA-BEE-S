Making a Difference, One Step at a Time

About Us

Nana-Bee's Foundation is dedicated to supporting orphans, the needy, and anyone in need of assistance. Through our initiatives, we aim to create a brighter future for individuals and communities by providing resources, opportunities, and a network of care and support. Together, we strive to make a difference, one life at a time.

Nana B Foundation Volunteer Training Workshop

Date:

Time: 12:00 pm - 5:00 pm

Duration: 5 hours


Objective:

The Nana B Foundation organized a day-long workshop training for its volunteers, focusing on enhancing their skills in accessing and supporting orphaned and vulnerable individuals.


Summary:

The workshop commenced with an address by the CEO, who emphasized the importance of dedication and compassion in impacting the lives of vulnerable persons. The CEO reiterated the foundation's commitment to supporting young vulnerable individuals and encouraged volunteers to make a positive difference through their contributions.


Training Sessions:

The main facilitator, Rexford, led the training sessions, which covered:

  • Screening and assessment of clients (orphans and vulnerable persons)
  • Registration processes for orphans and vulnerable individuals

Outcomes:

The workshop aimed to equip volunteers with the necessary skills and knowledge to effectively support the foundation's mission. The training sessions were interactive, and participants engaged actively with the facilitator.


Conclusion:

The Nana B Foundation Volunteer Training Workshop was a success, providing valuable insights and practical skills for the volunteers. The foundation remains committed to its mission of supporting vulnerable individuals and looks forward to continuing its work with the help of its dedicated volunteers.


Recommendations:

  • Regular training sessions to enhance volunteers' skills and knowledge
  • Continuous monitoring and evaluation of the foundation's programs and services
