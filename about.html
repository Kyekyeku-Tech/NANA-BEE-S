Welcome to Nana-Bee's Foundation

Nana-bee’s foundation is a unique non-governmental organization that commenced its establishment in september 2024 here in twifo hemang, central region, ghana.


The foundation has since done two (2) executive council meetings, one meeting with the board of governors and an in-service training for the volunteers.


The foundation is now registered with the registrar generals department – ghana and has obtained the business operating permit/certificate from the twifo hemang lower denkyira district assemly. Recruitment for community volunteers has been done with and in service training done for the purposes of beneficiaries recruitment.


Work on the recruitment process by the community volunteers are steadily ongoing. Outreach services by the ec to validate and observe the real time situation on the ground are also ongoing with pictorial/video evidence.

Our Vision: All identified needy children without basic needs have access to proper security, healthcare, and quality education.

What We Do

  • Support Services: We provide food, clothing, and essential care to orphans, the needy, and individuals facing hardship, ensuring their immediate needs are met.
  • Education and Training: We create opportunities for children and adults to access education and skills development, empowering them to build a brighter future.
  • Advocacy: Laise with people who are endowed to support the less privileged children in all disciplines.
  • Community Building: We foster connections through outreach programs, events, and collaborations to bring hope and encouragement to those in need.

Our mission Statement

Ensure every needy child is reached and has access to their basic needs, including but not limited to education, health, shelter, security, food and clothing.

Our Objective

  • To facilitate the provision of quality formal education services to the needy, orphans, less privileged and the vulnerable in order that they will reach their full potentials.


  • To recruit, assess and train community volunteers who will identify and report same on the orphans, vulnerable children and the hopeless youth in the communities for help.


  • To recruit health professionals and professionals from social workers department for the provission of better management and services to the beneficiaries.
